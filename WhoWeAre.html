We are a platform for food logistics

Our goal is to finally enable worry-free food logistics for both food companies and carriers. TWe are making food logistics efficient and transparent. We build a platform that simply and digitally brings together food companies with a network of specialized network of carriers. We provide central technological applications for management and control.

We help our partners to spare precious time and nerves during planning. We enable food companies to easily get goods from A to B whenever they need and support carriers to fulfill their full potential and let them work together in a network so they can compete with the large industry players.

Our combination of logistics and tech expertise together with our innovative and young startup spirit will make the food industry carefree and more profitable for everyone.

Today

Hi, I’m Flavio

founder of Fliit

I love logistics! I’ve spent the 16 years of my professional life in logistics, mostly at DHL, doing Packstation and other great projects. fliit actually brings together everything that I’ve learned and applies it to the champions league in logistics: food supply chain. I believe that logistics can be fun, should help businesses to grow, solve problems, need to provide peace of mind to shippers and care a lot more for its true rockstars, the carriers. That is why I founded fliit: To build the greatest platform in food logistics with the best carriers, helping shippers with transportation taking care of their products.
