Privacy Policy

General

This privacy policy informs you about the processing of your personal data via the website of Fliit Holding GmbH (" fliit "). fliit offers all shipping and transport partners the security in the food supply chain. And we are preparing the logistics ecosystem for the automation of the entire industry by using technology for every process we use.

We understand that you want your personal information to be protected. We know that protection and safety are very important to you. We will always treat your data in accordance with relevant data protection laws and regulations, such as the EU General Data Protection Regulation. This Privacy Policy informs you of how we collect, process and protect your personal information, for what purpose and for what legal reason we do so.

When using our website, fliit is recognized as the data controller for your personal data. When a company uses our services, we act solely as data processors. For more information about the processing of your personal data by applications of our customers, please refer to the privacy policy of the respective companies.

Our Website

We collect data through our website that may relate to you.

E-mail lists

If you have created an account on our website and you are a customer of fliit , we will send you emails to inform you about new features or other news regarding our company.

You can unsubscribe from this newsletter at any time or you can send us an e-mail to privacy@fliit.de so that we can do this for you.

In addition, we will gladly send you the necessary e-mails regarding your account or your personal payments.

Your account

To create an account, you must provide your name, company name, e-mail address and password. Please keep the combination of your e-mail address and your password secret. The name of your company becomes an organization, if it does not already exist on fliit . This organization becomes a parent company of your plans, invoices, conditions and settings. Let us know how you came upon us, so we know where to look for more ingenious clients like you. If you are already part of a team, you can join this team by clicking on a link or request an invitation from this team.

Our Cookies

We use certain cookies on our website. Cookies are small records sent by a website and stored by your web browser.

fliit

We use functional cookies. These are cookies, which you automatically log in if you want to, thus improving your browsing experience. We also use cookies that understand how you came to us on your first visit to our website, so that we can pay off relevant credits

Google Analytics

We use cookies from Google Analytics to collect information about the quality and effectiveness of this website. Your name or email address will never be shared with Google.

You can switch off the use of cookies automatically by adjusting the preferences and settings of your browser accordingly. Your experience on our website, however, may be affected.

Regarding the use of your personal data from fliit, you have the right:

  • to receive the confirmation if your data will be processed. And if that is the case, gain access to personal information and other related information;
  • correction of incorrect or incomplete personal data;
  • to remove your personal information if the processing is no longer useful, and / or you have withdrawn your consent to the processing of your personal information, and / or you object to the processing, and / or your personal data is processed unlawfully;
  • to restrict the processing of your personal data, if the accuracy of your personal data is denied, and / or your personal data are processed unlawfully, and / or your personal data are no longer required for fliit , but you need them in relation to legal claims, and / or you object to the processing and this objection is still being worked on;
  • to receive your personal information in a structured and machine-readable format so that it can be transmitted directly to another data processor.

fliit will respond to your inquiries within four weeks. If you do not agree with the contents of this document, the refusal of a request, or the processing of your personal data, you are entitled to submit a complaint to the German Data Protection Authority.

Third

Some of your data is shared with third-party services and our business partners.

Google

We use Google GSuite. All incoming e-mails are processed by us using Gmail and all relevant data, such as your e-mail address or any headers are kept by Google. We also use Google Drive to store spreadsheets and other documents that may include your name or email address.

Salesforce

We use Salesforce as our CRM system. This means we can keep data from shipping companies or freight leading companies that they shared with us when they became our customers or partners.

Freshdesk

We use Freshdesk as a service program. It works similar to an e-mail inbox. This means that it can retain data that helps to better support our customers. These data are removed after 180 days.

Atlassian

We use Atlassian Suite to manage our development process. We may retain data in order to use it to solve a problem or to assist our customers.

Bringg

We use Bringg as one of our disposition programs. Carriers can use Bringg to record and track the delivery process. Data of the recipients, such as name or address, are required to complete the delivery process and are processed by Bringg.

Onfleet

We also use Onflee as another of our disposition programs. Carriers can use Bringg to record and track the delivery process. Data of the recipients, such as name or address, are required to complete the delivery process and are processed by Onfleet.

Appsignal

We use AppSignal to monitor our processes and to track errors in the system. The error messages may include data of the recipients, such as name or address. That's how we can better support our customers. These data will be removed after 30 days.

Paper Trail

We use Papertrail to monitor our system logs. The protocols may include data of the recipients, such as name or address. That's how we can better support our customers. This data will be removed one year.

Zapier

We use Zapier to transfer our leads, which use the website of fliit. These data will be removed after three months.

Slack

We use Slack as an in-house communication program. With the support of our customers, messages with information about the recipients or emails can be sent internally by our customers and partners.Slack keeps 10,000 messages and then eliminates them automatically.

Webflow

We use Webflow to operate our website. When a user of our service submits a form, the information is first stored in Webflow and then transferred to our system.

We will only disclose your personal information if you have given us your express consent or if this is necessary to fulfill a contract, legal obligation or legitimate interests of fliit or if we are required to provide your personal information on the basis of a request or an order a competent authority.

If your personal information is shared with parties outside the European Union, we will ensure there is an adequacy decision by the European Commission or other appropriate safeguards have been put in place.

Safety measures

We have taken reasonable technical and organizational measures to ensure that your personal information is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws and regulations, and to ensure a high level of security and integrity of your personal information. All traffic is SSL (including communication between the fliit Data Collection Agent and our servers). We store the application data you sent to us in EU-US Privacy Shield, ISO 27001, ISO 27017, ISO 27018, PCI DSS Level 1 certified facilities in Ireland.
