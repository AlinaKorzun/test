Frequently Asked Questions

General

What are the advantages of working with fliit as a partner?

We relief you from all the hard and annoying stuff related to food logistics and help you to find the perfect solution particularly in short-haul transportation with our broad carrier partner network.

What is the difference between fliit and a traditional haulage company?

Especially with the special requirements of food logistics, traditional haulage companies have a very stiff setup and often can hardly handle singular requests. Based on our network and our asset-light model however, we can react to highly individual transport requests of any size quickly and flexibly

Can I only book the service of fliit within Germany?

At the moment fliit focuses on national food transportation. Nevertheless, feel free to talk to us about international requests and we will help you within our possibilities and provide you with useful advice.

Does the service of fliit cost?

The use of website, price calculator, counseling and contact to the team are completely free of charge.

Will my data be transmitted to third parties?

All data that we receive and collect from you will only be used to process and complete your requests and demanded services. That means your data will neither be sold nor leased to third parties or be provided for third party use for advertising or market research

Booking Process

How do I book a transportation with fliit?

Simply contact us at phone number or send a request to info@fliit.de. Alternatively, you can directly use the calculator on our website to have a price estimation for your transport calculated. We will contact you then to clarify all the details in a personalztalk.

What type of transportation can be executed through fliit?

We specialize in the transportation of any kind of food in distribution transportation. Nevertheless, with our broad carrier partner network that comprises many different vehicles we can also handle other requests in special cases.

Is there a minimum amount for transport requests?

No! Opposite to common haulage companies we can already handle requests starting from very small size such as one box, one EPAL or one roll cage. For more information please call us or send a request via e-mail and we talk about the possibilities and details with you.

Does fliit have a EU license?

Since we do not handle any vehicles or drive ourselves, the EU license does not apply to us. However, we ensure that all our carriers that execute your transport own a valid EU license

Does fliit have an IFS or comparable certification?

Since we do not own any vehicles or drive them ourselves, the IFS license does not apply to us. However, we ensure that our carrier partners have various certifications. Simply contact us at tel number or info@fliit.de to find out which certificates are represented in which region.

Booking Process

Who is my personal contact at fliit?

All of our clients receive their own personal contact who supports them during the whole project. Contact this person to talk about new project requests and issues concerning current projects as well as to ask any open questions.

What happens if my plans change on short notice?

No problem! Simply contact your personal contact at fliit or the disposition hotline directly and depending on the situation your transport will be redesigned based on your desires.

How can I reach out to fliit if there is the problem during my transport?

In case of incidents during your transport you can call our disposition at +49152 384 903 61. They will immediately answer all questions concerning your transport.
