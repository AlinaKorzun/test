How it works

Create a profile contact our sales team and provide us with your general information for easy request creation.

Create a transport request Inform us on times, location, delivery amount and special requirements for regular deliveries or spontaneous additional needs.

Receive offer We search the perfect carrier for you within our broad network of specialized carriers and suggest you the price instantly.

Provide details inform us and your carrier on exact pick up and drop of locations.

Lean back we take over from now on and make sure that the carrier receives the correct information, picks up and delivers you goods as requested. In addition, we solve any incidents that may come up and provide alternatives before you can even start stressing. Of course, we will inform you on any incidents immediately anyway.

Track transport and receive documentation follow the progress of your delivery through our track and trace system and check exact delivery time, temperature, and other KPIs within the dashboard. Don’t wait for proofs of delivery but view it digitally instantly.
